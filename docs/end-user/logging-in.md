# Logging In

---

## How to Log In

1. Open your web browser and go to the dashboard URL provided by your administrator (e.g. `https://yourdomain.com`).

2. You will be shown the **Login** page.

3. Enter your **email address** and **password** in the fields provided.

4. Optionally tick **Remember me** to stay logged in across browser sessions.

5. Click **Login**.

6. If your credentials are correct, you will be automatically redirected to the view that matches your account type:
   - **Standard User** → your station map
   - **Group User** → your group's station map
   - **Technician** → the all-stations map
   - **Admin** → the administration panel

---

## If Your Login Fails

The page will show a red error message. Common causes:

| Error message | Likely cause | What to do |
|--------------|-------------|-----------|
| "Invalid email or password" | Typo in email or password | Double-check caps lock; try again |
| Page refreshes with no message | Email does not exist in the system | Contact your administrator |
| "Too many attempts" (if enabled) | Account temporarily locked | Wait a few minutes or contact admin |

There is **no self-service password reset**. If you have forgotten your password, contact your administrator. They can delete and re-create your account, or update the password directly in the database.

---

## Logging Out

Every view has a **Logout** button:
- On map views it appears in the left sidebar.
- On the admin panel it appears at the top of the screen.

Click it and you will be returned to the login page. Your session is cleared immediately.

---

## Signing Up

If a **Sign Up** link is visible on the login page, new users can self-register. After signing up, your account will have no permissions assigned — you will see a blank screen until an administrator assigns you a permission type.

If the Sign Up link is not visible, registration has been disabled. Contact your administrator to have an account created for you.

---

## Changing Your Password

There is no in-app password change form. Ask your administrator to update your password. Admins can do this via the **Admin Panel → Users** list — see [admin.md](admin.md) for their instructions.
